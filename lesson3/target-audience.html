Target Audience

Personas

Bill the Business Owner

Bill is a small business owner in the Santaquin Area. He does a lot of business through word of mouth and referals

Sally the Citizen

Sally is a citizen in the city of Santaquin. She likes to support local businesses and she likes to be involved in community activities and affairs. She is politically involved and aware.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Bill is coming to the website to see what businesses are already here. He wants to know if there are any competing businesses in the nearby area that he doesn't know about.

Scenario 2

Bill's business isn't listed on the chamber of commerce website yet. He thinks it would really help him out if the city could help him promote his small business.

Scenario 3

Sally just moved to the area but because of her busy work life she hasn't had a chance to explore the community yet. She comes to the website to see a few of the businesses around.

Scenario 4

Sally has noticed a lot of new growth on the north end of town and wants to know what businesses are in that part of town now.

Scenario 5

Sally is an avid supporter of local businesses. She has been buying all of her fruit at Walmart but wants to find a way she can get fruit from the local farmers.
