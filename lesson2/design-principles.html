Design Principles Document

Emily Wayment

White Space and Clean Design

Boise Web

White space and clean design when used correctly makes pages cleaner and easier to understand. Create space around elements and don't use too many words. Space things out and give proper attention to things that need it.

PARC: Alignment

PureNature Pest Control

Alignment means that things aren't placed randomly on the page. Make a grid, invisible or not, or make sure things have a connections with another element.

PARC: Contrast

Cherry Hill Farms

Use contrast to make things stand out and make things readable. No one wants to visit a page they can't read. This can also be used to get someone's attention. Create emphasis on things that should be emphasized.
